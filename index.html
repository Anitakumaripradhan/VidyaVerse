Executive Summary

The #DataDreamer campaign across Instagram, YouTube, and LinkedIn generated massive clicks and impressions. However, true success is measured by student signups and course completions. This analysis dives into metrics like Cost Per Signup (CPS), Click-Through Rate (CTR), and average completion percentage to evaluate the true effectiveness of the campaign on each platform.

Key Insights

  • Instagram: Leads in completion percentage (58.14%) and delivers the lowest cost per conversion, indicating the highest ROI and most engaged students.
  • LinkedIn: Attracts a high number of motivated, professional learners and shows the highest raw conversion rate, but at a significantly higher cost.
  • YouTube: Effective for brand awareness and reach, but less efficient than other platforms in driving quality conversions and completions.
  • Overall Performance: With a 42% average completion rate, VidyaVerse is performing significantly above the industry benchmark in India (5-15%).

Recommendations

  • Invest in Instagram: Increase ad spend on Instagram as it provides the best return on investment and highest quality student engagement.
  • Maintain LinkedIn Presence: Reduce spend but continue to leverage LinkedIn for its strong professional student base.
  • Use YouTube for Reach: Keep YouTube in the marketing mix for top-of-funnel brand awareness.
  • Enhance Platform with AI: Integrate AI-driven features like chatbots for doubt clearance and lecture summaries to boost engagement and ROI further.
